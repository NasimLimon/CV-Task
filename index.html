Tools: [Java,OOP concept]

Flappy Bird is a simple yet highly addictive game where the player taps the screen to keep a small bird flying between narrow pipes. Each tap makes the bird flap upward, while gravity pulls it downward. The goal is to pass through as many gaps as possible without hitting the pipes or the ground. Its challenging mechanics and minimalist design made Flappy Bird a global phenomenon.
